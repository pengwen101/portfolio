# Sumatra Flood 2025: Public Perceptions of the Indonesian Government’s Disaster Management

![alt text](/img/sumatra_topic_model.png)

This undergraduate research project, supervised by a university professor, explores the intersection of digital technology and sustainability. I analyzed over 30,000 YouTube comments regarding a 2025 natural disaster in Sumatra. Through this analysis, I identified patterns and sentiment polarity among state actors, as well as a high correlation between government and environmental topics (further details can be found in the published paper). Topic modeling was conducted using BERTopic, and I utilized statistical methods such as Chi-squared tests and Pearson correlation.
